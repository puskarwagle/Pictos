## भाग २: वैज्ञानिक खोज

ADHD bhako manche ko dimaag ma dopamine kam huncha.
Yo nai tyo chemical ho jo motivation, impulse control, ra good feel, relaxed garna help garcha.

Yo kasari thaha bhayo? Vigyan ko yatra


1950 tira Amphetamine naamak uttejak ausadhi hyperactive chakchake bachcha harulai lagbhag durghatana wash diyiyo. Natija achammako thiyo — uttejak ausadhile uniharulai shanta banayo. Yo pahilo sanket thiyo — yadi uttejak padartha le shanta banauncha bhane, dimaag pahile nai kam uttejit awasthama thiyo. Baigyanik harule ausadhi bata pachhadi farkera mechanism khojna thale.

Tyo chemical dopamine. Motivation, focus, khusi — sab. ADHD dimaag ma yo kam huncha.

Yo mero bahana hoina. Yo kunai ko ray hoina. 
Yo jivit maanaw dimaag bhitra **dekhieko** kura ho.

## भाग ३: परिणामहरू

### उनीहरूले किन अरूलाई दुःख दिन्छन्

ADHD bhaka manis haru sanga sangai huna kahilekahi wastawamai gaaro huncha. Uniharu kharab manche bhayera hoina. Tara uniharuka lakshan harule wariparika manis haruma wastawik asar parcha.

**Birsiyeka bachaharu** — niyat kharab nahola, tara asar wastawik huncha.
**Bhawnatmak bisphot** — PFC ko brake bina amygdala sakriy hunu. Sano aalochanama pani ati pratikriya. Jasle pachhi teshko barema dherai naramro mahasus garcha tara tyo chhyanma rokna sakena.
**Asthirta** — ek din nikai tej, arko din purntaya anupasthit. D2 autoreceptor instability ho, tara bahirabata waist na gareko jasto dekhincha.
**Naya kurama hyperfocus, purana pratibaddhata haruko tyag** — naya project ma 14 ghanta. Tara bidyamaan jimmedari le kehi paudaina.
**Arthik bhadragol** — dopamine khojima aabegpurna kharcha. Billa haru birsincha. Pariwar lai asar garcha.

---

### परिणामहरू (The Downstream Consequences)

Yahan aayera "farak hunu" khataranak bancha. ADHD aafaaima khataranak bhayera hoina — tara sahayog napako ADHD mastishka yasko lagi nabaneko sansarma rahanda anumanit ra binashakari parinamharu nimtyauncha.

**Depression:** ADHD bhaka karib 70% manis harule jiwan kalma depression ko anubhav garchan. Nirantar ko asafalta, laaj, masking bata thakan, ra pahile dekhi nai asantulit dopamine pranali. Depression ra ADHD bich mesolimbic pathway ma neurobiological samanta huncha — eutale arkolai jhanai bigarcha.

**Anxiety:** Karib 50% ADHD bayasak haruma anxiety disorder sangai huncha. Sadhai asafal huna lageko darma baanchnu, aafnai mastishka lai biswas garna nasaknu. Sharir sadhai kam starko stress response ma rahancha. HPA axis — tanaw harmone pranali — dopamine pranali sangai asantulit huncha.

**Lat:** ADHD bhaka manis haruma lagupadarth durbyasan ko sambhawana 2-3 guna badhi huncha. Dopamine ko bhoko mastishka le jahan bhetincha tehin bata dopamine khojcha — raksi, ganja, nicotine. Yo kamjori hoina — self-medication ho. Dukhad kura: dherai le diagnosis hunu bhanda pahile nai thaha pauchan ki kehi padarth le aafailai ramro sanga kaam garna maddat garcha.

**Bigriyeka sambandh haru:** ADHD bhaka bayasak haruko sambandha bichhed hune sambhawana jhandai dui guna badhi huncha. Bhawnatmak asthirta, birsiyeka pratibaddhata, arthik bhadragol — dherai sambandha haru ko lagi yo laamo samaya samma jhelna gaaro huncha.

**Berojgari ra arthik asthirta:** ADHD bhaka bayasak harule ausatma neurotypical sathi haruko tulana ma nikai kam kamauchan. Jagir bata nikaline sambhawana badhi. Degree pura garne sambhawana kam.

**Sarsafai ra aatma-herchaha:** Daant manjne, nuhaune, niyamit khane — yi sabai ko lagi task initiation, routine maintenance, ra time awareness chaincha. Tinnai ADHD ma kamjor hunchan. Yo alchhipan hoina — yo uhi neurological differences ko pratyaksha lakshan ho.

**Kanuni jhamela ra jail:** Anuman anusar jail ma raheka 25-40% manis haruma ADHD cha, jabaki samanya janasankhyama yo matra 5-7% cha. Impulsivity, parinam ko kamjor mulyankan — yi charitra ka dosh hoinan, yi lakshan hun.

**Bidroh:** Wastawamai purna prayas gari raheko mastishka lai barshaun samma "timi alchhi hau, murkha hau" bhaniye pachhi aakrosh paida huncha. Jayaj aakrosh. Kehi le bhitratira modchan — depression, self-harm. Kehi le bahirtira — authority rejection, bidroh. Yo personality disorder hoina — jiwanbhar nabujhieko karanle paida bhako trauma response ho.

**Aatmahatya:** ADHD bhaka bayasak harule neurotypical bayasak haruko tulana ma 2-4 guna badhi aatmahatya ko prayas garne sambhawana huncha. Depression, emotional dysregulation, impulsivity, ra nirantar ko laaj ko mishran. Atyadhik bhawnatmak tanawo ko chhyan ma — amygdala sakriy, PFC banda — auta aabegpurna nirnaya ghatak huna sakcha.

**Choto aayu:** Professor Russell Barkley ko anusandhan le patta lagayo ki gambhir ADHD jiwan pratyasha **8 dekhi 13 barsale** ghatne sanga jodieko cha. Eutai karan le hoina — durghatana, lat, aatmahatya, dirghakalin chinta bata hune mutu ko samasya, ra dashakaun samma crisis mode ma chaleko sharir.

Yahi nai upachar nabhayeko, pahichan nabhayeko, sahayog napako ADHD ko jiwanbhar ko tasbir ho. Yo kunai ramailo byaktitwa biseshata hoina. Yo ek neurological awastha ho jaslai sambodhan nagariye ma wastawik, mapanyogya, binashakari parinam haru aauchan.

Ra Nepal ma — jahan ADHD bhanne sabda sayedai kasailai thaha cha, jahan uplabdh ekmatra bhasha "alchhi," "murkha," "prayas nagarne" bhanne matra cha — yi pratyek parinam haru ahile ti manis haruma bhai raheka chan jaslai aafu kina yasto chu bhanne attopatto chaina.

## भाग ४: अन्त्य

Ma yo chij le chapaieko chu. Mero kshamata ra mero uplabdhi bich ko khadal. Asafaltaharu. Laaj haru. Tara biteko bitisakyo.

Aaj dhilo bhayo — tara dherai dhilo bhako chaina.

Kripaya — ausadhi unban garnuhos. Amphetamine ra Methylphenidate Nepal ma uplabdh garaunuhos. Auta Ameriki ko sharir ra auta Nepali ko sharir eutai ho. Yo paschimi kura ho bhanera nabhan nus. Yo maanaw mastishka ko kura ho.

Amphetamine ra Methylphenidate (Ritalin) duwai le synapse ma dopamine badhaunchan. Methylphenidate le DAT lai block garcha. Amphetamine le DAT block garnu ka sathai dopamine jabardasti bahir nikalcha. Duwai ADHD ma kaam garchan. Nishkarsh: Dopamine ko upalabdhata nai mukhya samasya ho.

Ra jo manche yo ausadhi durupayog garchan bhanera malai mero ausadhi bata rokchau — tyo tarka galat cha. Sleeping pills niyantran garna sakchau. Yaslai pani gara. Niyantran gara. Niyaman gara.

Malai baanki jindagi ramro mastishka le baanchna deu. Gahiro sambandha banauna deu. Ramro sanga kaam garna deu. Ramro chhora, daaju, saathi banna deu. Ramro nagarik banna deu.

Gara. Kripaya.
