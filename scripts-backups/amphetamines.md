**EK CHOTI TABLET. TAPAIKO BRAIN MA KE HUNCHHA?**
*(Amphetamine — The Science Inside)*

---

Namaste. Aaja hami ek yesto cheez ko kura garnechhau — jasto tapai sochnuhunchha, "yo ta sirf ek tablet ho." Sirf ek tablet. Paani sanga nilnuhunchha. Sajilo. Simple.

Tara tyo tablet tapai ko sharir bhitra pugepachi... kura complicated hunchha.

---

Pehila — stomach. Tyo tablet stomach ma pugchha. Stomach ko acid le coating galaaunchha. Ani tablet dissolve huna thalchha. Yo process roughly 30 minutes jati lagna sakchha.

Stomach bhanda tala — small intestine. Yo nai main absorption point ho. Yahaan bata drug tapai ko bloodstream ma enter garchha. Seedha blood ma. Aba yo drug tapai sanga travel garchha — pump pump pump — heart le push gardai.

Tara seedha brain pugdaina. Pahile — liver.

---

Liver. Tapai ko body ko bouncer. Ekdam strict. Jo pani blood bata aauchha — pahile mero saamne aaunus bhancha. Ani jun drug aayo, tesko ek significant portion liver le nai break down garchha. Destroy garchha. Waste banaaunchha.

Yeslai bhaninchha — First Pass Metabolism. Tapai le jati drug khanubhayepani, sab brain pugdaina. Kehi ta liver le nai hataidinchha.

Tara amphetamine — yo ek "smart" drug ho. Liver bata bachchha. Enough amount bloodstream ma survive garchha.

---

Aba bloodstream bata brain samma pugnu chha. Tara brain — yo alag nai level ko security ho. Blood Brain Barrier. BBB.

Sochnus — brain euta VIP club ho. Strong security. Guest list chha. Tapai ko body ko dherai chemicals lai — "no entry."

Virus? No.
Bacteria? No.
Random molecules? Absolutely no.

Tara amphetamine? Yo guest list ma parchha. Kina? Kina bhane yo lipid-soluble chha, ani sano molecule ho. Brain ko security door bata slip bhayera bhitra paschha.

Aba game suru hunchha.

---

Tapai ko brain ma neurons chhan — arba kharab neurons. Tiniharule communicate garchhan chemicals bata. Tyo chemicals lai bhaninchha neurotransmitters.

Dui wota main neurotransmitters — dopamine ra norepinephrine.

Dopamine — yo "reward" chemical ho. Tapai le ramro khaana khanubho? Dopamine. Tapai le kaam saknubho? Dopamine. Social media ma appreciation paunubho? Dopamine.

Norepinephrine — yo "alert" chemical ho. Focus. Energy. Heart rate badhaaunchha. Body lai ready state ma rakhchha.

Normally, yo chemicals release hunchhan. Kaam garchhan. Ani feri reuptake hunchha — matlab neuron le tyo chemical feri absorb garchha. Recycle. Clean slate.

Yo system normally balanced hunchha.

---

Amphetamine le yo balance chaos ma lyauchha.

Pehila — neurons lai force garchha massive amount of dopamine ra norepinephrine release garna. Normal release bhanda dherai badi.

Dosro — reuptake block garchha. Matlab tyo chemicals baahira nai rahanchhan. Recycle hudaina. Synapse ma flood hunchha.

Result? Tapai ko brain suddenly huge amount of dopamine ma dubeko jasto hunchha.

Ani tapai ko experience? Sharp focus. Everything makes sense jasto feel hunchha. Productive feel hunchha. Confident. Alert. "Aaja sab kaam sakchhu" wala feeling.

Heart rate badhchha. Blood pressure badhchha. Appetite ghatna sakchha. Bhok kam lagna sakchha.

---

Tara yo forever rakhna mildaina.

Jaba drug wear off hunchha — dopamine depleted hunchha. Stores khaali jasto hunchha. Brain lai normal kaam garna garo hunchha.

Crash. Exhaustion. Irritability. Low mood. "Kina ma yesto feel gardai chhu?" wala state.

Ani yahi point bata dependence ko risk badhna sakchha — kina bhane brain le tyo dopamine flood yaad rakhchha, ani feri tyahi state khojna sakchha.

---

Aba important kura — amphetamine ko medical use pani chha. Yeslai dismiss nagarnus.

ADHD — Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder. Yo condition ma brain ko attention ra dopamine systems differently function garna sakchhan. Focus garna garo hunchha. Task complete garna garo hunchha. Overstimulation huna sakchha.

ADHD bhayeko manche lai controlled dose ma stimulant medications — jastai Adderall, Vyvanse, Ritalin — le actual balance create garna sakchha.

Counterintuitive lagna sakchha — stimulant le calm garchha? Tara ADHD brain ko lagi, yo normalization huna sakchha, hyperactivation hoina.

Correctly diagnosed, correctly dosed, doctor ko supervision ma — yo medication ho.

---

Tara prescription bina? Recreational use? "Study drug" ko roop ma? Yo kura alag ho.

Long-term misuse le dopamine system dysregulate garna sakchha. Dependency, tolerance, withdrawal jasta risks badhna sakchhan.

Repeated misuse over time le brain function ma lasting changes lyau­na sakchha. Yo dramatic kura hoina. Yo biology ho.

---

Yo nai kura ho — ek choti tablet. Stomach. Liver. Blood. Brain. Dopamine flood. Focus. Crash. Ani misuse bhaye — much longer story.

Tapai ko brain — lagbhag 1.4 kg ko euta organ — tyo nai tapai ho. Tyo nai tapai ko memories, personality, decisions.

Tyasaile chemistry sanga khelnu lightly linu hudaina.
