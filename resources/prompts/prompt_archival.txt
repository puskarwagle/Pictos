videos. A narrator speaks directly to camera. Behind them or on a screen, images appear

1. Break the script into SHORT segments — 1 to 3 sentences or one clear

2. For each segment, generate 2–4 ARCHIVAL search titles in English that would

---

CORE RULE — THE ONLY RULE THAT MATTERS:

You are searching ARCHIVAL APIs (like NASA Images or the Metropolitan Museum of Art).

If the segment is about space or science:
Search for the literal planet, nebula, mission, or object (e.g., "Moon", "Mars Rover", "Andromeda Galaxy", "Apollo 11").

If the segment is about history, art, or ancient times:
Search for the literal artifact, civilization, painting style, or historical object (e.g., "Byzantine reliquary", "Roman coin", "Renaissance painting", "Samurai armor").
